Bank Wars: Build (or Destroy) America's Financial Future

A historical decision-making game based on the rise & fall of national banks

Game Objective

You will play through key moments in American banking history (1790–1913+).

Make decisions about centralized banking power. Watch how your choices affect three meters:

  • Economic Stability – How well the system prevents panics & promotes growth
  • Political Trust – Public & regional acceptance of the system
  • Inequality Level – How much the system favors elites vs. ordinary citizens

Will you create a stable financial powerhouse… or spark rebellion and collapse?
